Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Log Data is only used to understand how a crash occurred and how to fix it. It is not sent to other third-party services. Log Data is only a snapshot of VNDBA's state at the time a crash occurs: it is not collected, nor is it updated when VNDBA doesn't crash. This information is anonymous: your username is not collected in Log Data. However, Log Data may include but is not limited to:

  • The number of VNs you have in your VN list : this helps us determine the size of your account, which may be a factor of crash.
  • Your preferences within the app.
  • The pages you visited, and the page you were watching when the crash occurred : only the page's name is collected, not its content. If you visited a VN's page, its ID is also collected.
You can opt-out of Log Data at anytime in your preferences. By default, Log Data is disabled.
